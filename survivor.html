DEAD BY DAYLIGHT

Survivors

Dwight Fairfield is a nervous Leader, able to locate his allies and increase their effectiveness. His personal perks, Bond, Prove Thyself and Leader, allow him to get in proximity of other Survivors and provide bonuses to their actions as well as his own.

Meg Thomas is an energetic athlete, able to outrun most Killers. Her personal perks, Quick & Quiet, Sprint Burst and Adrenaline, give her advantages to escape chases and put distance between herself and the Killer.

Claudette Morel is a studious Botanist, able to boost her team's survivability as well as her own. Her personal Perks, Empathy , Botany Knowledge , and Self-Care. , allow her to spot injured Survivors and heal both herself and her team more effectively.
