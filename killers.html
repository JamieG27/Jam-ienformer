DEAD BY DAYLIGHT

.Trapper

The Trapper is an area-control Killer, able to apply pressure across the map by placing deadly Bear Traps for Survivors to step into. His personal perks, Unnerving Presence, Brutal Strength and Agitation, allow him to chase and carry Survivors more effectively.

.Wraith

The Wraith is a stealth Killer, able to cloak and uncloak at the ringing of his Wailing Bell. His personal perks, Predator, Bloodhound and Shadowborn, give him extra abilities to track and locate Survivors, making him a very efficient Hunter.
